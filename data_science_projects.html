Projects

Pittsburgh Neighborhood Safety & Amenities Analysis

Summary: I employed a tri-metric approach to assess neighborhood safety in Pittsburgh. To support this, I developed a Python script utilizing pandas to assign weighted rankings to neighborhoods based on the diversity of playground equipment manufacturers. Furthermore, I leveraged numpy and matplotlib to undertake a comprehensive data analysis, culminating in insightful visual representations of the findings

Credit Card Fraud Detection

Summary: I innovatively engineered and fine-tuned an ML-based fraud detection system, leveraging models such as SVM, Random Forest, and XGBoost. To tackle data imbalance, I employed techniques like SMOTE, ensuring more accurate predictions. Moreover, by instituting real-time monitoring and performance metrics, I significantly enhanced fraud detection capabilities and notably reduced false positive rates.
